# **App Name**: Drift Coffee

## Core Features:

- Cinematic Hero Scroll Animation: Implement a scroll-driven, cinematic animation of an iced coffee cup using a WebP frame sequence (frame_000_delay-0.041s.webp to frame_118_delay-0.041s.webp, total 119 frames) located at 'https://mnjjesosykmgfmnfbwaz.supabase.co/storage/v1/object/sign/coffee/frame_'. The animation maps scroll position to frame index, ensuring smooth transitions and slow, premium pacing.
- Interactive Parallax Scrolling: Develop a multi-layered parallax effect on the hero section where the coffee cup moves at normal speed, the background gradient moves slower, and optional details like droplets move slightly faster to create a sense of depth.
- Elegant Product Showcase: Design a product section that reuses the static coffee visual, presenting it with a centered composition, a bold minimal background, and a clean call-to-action button for exploring the menu.
- Premium Preloader Experience: Create a minimal loading screen displaying 'Drift Coffee' and a smooth progress bar to preload initial hero animation frames, ensuring a seamless entry experience without flickering.
- Performance Optimized Design: Ensure optimal website performance by utilizing WebP images, implementing lazy loading for animation frames, and optimizing rendering to achieve a smooth user experience that feels approximately 60 frames per second.
- AI-Enhanced Flavor Description Tool: Integrate a generative AI tool to craft rich, evocative tasting notes and sensory descriptions for various coffee selections, enhancing the perception of premium quality and unique flavors.

## Style Guidelines:

- The dominant color scheme is light, reflecting a warm, premium coffee aesthetic. The primary color is a rich, dark caramel-brown (#8F5924) providing elegance and contrast. The background is a very light, creamy off-white (#EBE6E0), which offers a warm and inviting canvas. An accent color, a deep, warm red-orange (#C34B22), is used sparingly for call-to-actions to provide a refined pop.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clean, and highly readable characteristics, aligning with an Apple-level minimalist and luxurious aesthetic.
- Use minimalist, outline-based icons rendered in monochromatic tones to complement the clean typography and luxury product-focused design, avoiding any elaborate or overly decorative elements.
- Implement an Apple-level minimalist layout with ample spacing between elements and sections to create an airy, premium feel. Content will be primarily centered, establishing a clear visual hierarchy, with specific hero sections featuring a split left-right composition.
- All animations across the site, including hover states and page transitions, should be smooth, slow, and premium, avoiding fast or dramatic motions. Specific attention will be given to interpolation and smoothing for scroll-driven elements to maintain a cinematic, controlled feel.
